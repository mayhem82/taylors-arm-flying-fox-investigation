Tourism asset

The supplementary pathway records the Pub With No Beer as a state-promoted tourism asset and a nationally recognised cultural destination.

Exposure

The pathway identifies outdoor dining, camping, festival activity, visitor amenity and the surrounding village precinct as the commercial exposure surface.

Recipient

Destination NSW is identified as the additional recipient because its function is tourism and economic development rather than environment or biosecurity.

Classification

The tourism pathway operates as a conditional projected pathway. It depends on the colony remaining unmanaged through a future peak visitor period or comparable tourism exposure point.

Supplementary pathway. Does not replace the primary investigation package.
